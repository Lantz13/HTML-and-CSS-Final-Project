Fitness Classes

Zumba

Zumba® Fitness is a feel-happy workout that combines a motivating fusion of Latin and international music with unique moves and dynamic combinations that allow participants to dance away their worries. It is based on the principle that a workout should be ‘Fun and Easy to Do’ allowing Zumba® Fitness participants to stick to the program and achieve long-term health benefits in both body and mind.

Strength & Conditioning

Challenge yourself with this athletic based cardio and strength class. You may work your way through a variety of stations or you might be challenged within your own space. Either way this boot camp style class will give you a complete body workout.

Pound

Pound fitness is a full-body cardio jam session, combining light resistance with constant simulated drumming. The workout fuses cardio, Pilates, isometric movements, plyometrics and Isometric poses into a 45-minute series. Burn between 400 and 900+ calories per hour, strengthen and sculpt infrequently used muscles, and drum your way to a leaner, slimmer physique – all while rocking out to your favorite music!

Cycling

The Real Ryder Indoor Cycling bike is like no other indoor bike on the planet. Our unique, patented articulating frame technology lets the rider- any rider, novice to pro- fully engage their body and mind in a one-of-a-kind tactile, dynamic and functional riding experience. This class has the option to ride the bike for a full hour or begin the class on the bike and end with strength and conditioning.

Yoga

This class is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines. It originated in ancient India, aimed at controlling and stilling the mind, and recognizing the detached 'witness-consciousness' as untouched by the activities of the mind and mundane suffering.

HIIT

HIIT - High-intensity interval training is a form of interval training, a cardiovascular exercise strategy alternating short periods of intense anaerobic exercise with less intense recovery periods, until too exhausted to continue. This class is a full body intense workout!
